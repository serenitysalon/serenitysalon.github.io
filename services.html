Deep Pore Cleansing Facial
An intensive facial uses a combination of products that is perfect for anyone with congested pores. Although your skin may appear clean on the outside, the surface can contain a buildup of cellular debris, makeup residue, and dead skin cells. These can clog your pores and keep your skin from looking and feeling its best.
Ultra Hydration Facial
An elegant treatment that begins with a gentle hydrating, cleansing, and micro-exfoliation to remove surface debris. The skin is then prepped for a triple action resurfacing process to enhance brightness and clarity, and address skin texture, fine lines, and wrinkles. ZO® Aloe Hydra gel is applied to cool, calm, and soothe the skin, followed by pressure point massage manipulations to minimize facial stress and maximize relaxation.
Swedesh Massage
Swedish massage is one of the most commonly offered massage techniques. It's sometimes called a classic massage. The technique aims to promote relaxation by releasing muscle tension. Swedish massage is gentler than deep tissue massage and better suited for people interested in relaxation and tension relief.
Thai Massage
This deep heat medicinal muscle treatment dates back to the 14th century. Our therapists will apply a warm herbal compress filled with organic herbs directly to the body with a variety of specialized techniques.
Hot Stone Massage
A natural therapy is known for its healing capabilities by using warmed basalt stones. The smooth, flat stones are placed on key points of the body to create heat penetration that helps ease tension.
